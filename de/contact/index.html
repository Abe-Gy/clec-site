Found a Bug?

As CLEC.live is currently in beta, we really appreciate bug reports. They help us make the platform better for everyone. When reporting a bug, it helps to include:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Any error messages you saw
  • The device and browser you were using

Report a bug at feedback@clec.live

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days. Thank you for your patience!
