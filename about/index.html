About CLEC.live

Create Live Events Collaboratively

Our Mission

CLEC.live is a platform built to make live events easy and accessible. Our main goal is simple: bring people together to socialise and have fun.

We believe that the best memories are made when people come together for live experiences. Whether it's a jazz concert in a cozy bar, a comedy night at a local venue, or a full-scale music festival – these moments matter.

What We Do

For Collaborators

We make it super easy for artists, venues, and contributors in the industry – like sound engineers, caterers, cinematographers, and more – to collaborate and create amazing events together.

For Audiences

We make it super easy for audiences to discover, reach, and attend these events. Finding your next great experience should be effortless.

The ultimate goal is that people get together and make cool memories.

Who We Are

CLEC.live is a brand new platform created by live music-loving people to serve the community. We're passionate about live events and understand the challenges that come with organizing them. That's why we built this platform – to make collaboration seamless and event creation joyful.

Beta Version

CLEC.live is currently in beta testing. Feel free to use the platform free of charge during this period. While we work hard to provide a smooth experience, you may encounter occasional bugs. If you discover any issues or have feature suggestions, please reach out to us at feedback@clec.live.
